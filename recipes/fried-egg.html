Fried Egg

Home

Description

Fried Egg is one of God's most perfect creations. I think it's the best food in the world. One time I was eating 2 eggs a day and threw up on the 30th day of doing this. That is how dedicated I am.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (as many as you like)
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl and stir with a fork until the whites are mixed with the yellow.
  2. Warm your skillet and put about 1tbsp of oil in there.
  3. Put your egg in the warm skillet and oil.
  4. Wait for it to solidify a little, I'd cook it on low if this is your first time.
  5. Eat it! So good!!
