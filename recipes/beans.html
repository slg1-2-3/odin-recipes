Description

Red Beans and Rice is a classic southern dish that has saved my life multiple times.
It's a savory dish that consists of beans, chicken stock, rice, onions, peppers, garlic and sausage
The dish is flexible, so if you mess it up, that's ok. It will still taste pretty good!

Ingredients

  • Seasonings
    • Onion Powder
    • Garlic Powder
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper
    • Cayenne
    • Smoked Paprika
  • Carbs, Vegetables, and Protein
    • Beans
    • Rice
    • Andoulie Sausage
    • Chicken Stock
    • Onion
    • Jalapeno Pepper
    • Garlic

Steps

  1. First step is to soak your beans overnight in salt water
  2. Dice onion and jalapeno, mince the garlic
  3. Cut sausage into dice-sized cubes
  4. Mix equal parts of each seasoning into a bowl
  5. Light fire in large pot and lightly cover the bottom with oil, throw in cubed sausagege to cook first
  6. Throw in onions, jalapenos. Allow them to cook down until fragrant, then throw in 1/6th of your seasoning
  7. Add beans and chicken stock and 4/6ths of your seasoning. Mix well.
  8. Let simmer for 2hrs.
  9. Taste. Add seasoning if you need it
